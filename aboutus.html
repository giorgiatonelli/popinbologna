ABOUT US

This project was developed by 6 girls who attend the class of Information and Technology Skills of the Language, Society and Communication Master's Degree at the University of Bologna and are united by the passion of studying languages and a shared interest in music.
You can explore our website with its sections divided by topic that will help you better to focus on our different investigations.
Here, you will find 3 stories about various aspects of the musical world:
  1. Pop in Bologna, developed by Giorgia Tonelli and Camilla Guarneri
  2. Pink Floyd, developed by Martina Santarini and Alessia Perrone
  3. Red Hot Chili Peppers, developed by Alessandra Greco and Valentina Marcucci
